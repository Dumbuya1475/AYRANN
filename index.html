About Ayrann

Abdul Salam Kamara, known professionally as AYRANN, is a Sierra Leonean singer and songwriter.

  • Born: July 9, 2007 (age 17 years), Freetown
  • Genre: Afrobeats, R&B, Afropop, Drill
  • Instrumental: Vocals
  • Origin: Freetown, Sierra Leone
  • Other names: theyluv_ayran, ayran
